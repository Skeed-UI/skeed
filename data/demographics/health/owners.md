## AAA-strict requirements
- WCAG 2.1 AAA (7:1 contrast for all text)
- No PHI in URLs, localStorage, or analytics events (HIPAA-adjacent)
- Tap targets minimum 48px (motor impairment consideration)
- Plain language at grade 6–8 reading level
- Emergency contact (911) accessible from all health-critical flows
- Tag PRs with `aaa-strict` and `health-domain`
