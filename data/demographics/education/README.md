# Education

Educational technology for K-12, higher education, and lifelong learning. Focus on accessibility, inclusive design, and supporting diverse learning styles.

## Design Rationale

Education demographics serve users from ages 5-18 plus adult educators. The design system prioritizes:

- **AAA Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AAA compliance for students with disabilities
- **Cognitive Load Management**: Clear, simple interfaces that minimize distraction
- **Inclusive Representation**: Diverse imagery and content that reflects all learners
- **Privacy & Safety**: COPPA-compliant, transparent data practices
- **Progress Visibility**: Clear feedback on learning progress and achievements
- **Parental Engagement**: Supporting family involvement in education

## Niches

- **K-12**: Primary and secondary education with strong parental controls
- **Higher Ed**: University and college platforms with research integration
- **Lifelong Learning**: Adult education and professional development

## Key Principles

1. **Encouraging Tone**: Support intrinsic motivation without manipulative gamification
2. **Clear Instructions**: Explicit, jargon-free guidance for all ages
3. **Consistent Navigation**: Predictable patterns reduce cognitive overhead
4. **Feedback Loops**: Timely, specific feedback on learning progress
5. **Safety First**: Protecting student data and privacy is paramount
