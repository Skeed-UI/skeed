# Mental Wellness

Digital mental health, therapy platforms, and wellbeing applications. Designed with trauma-informed principles and crisis accessibility.

## Design Rationale

Mental wellness serves users in vulnerable emotional states. The design prioritizes:

- **Crisis Accessibility**: Immediate access to crisis resources, prominently displayed
- **Privacy & Trust**: Explicit privacy protections, clear consent flows, HIPAA awareness
- **Calming Aesthetic**: Soft colors, gentle motion, spacious layouts to reduce anxiety
- **Non-Judgmental Tone**: Empathetic, supportive language without shame or pressure
- **AAA Accessibility**: Essential for users with cognitive differences or disabilities
- **Human Connection**: Supporting therapeutic relationships, not replacing them

## Niches

- **Therapy**: Telehealth platforms, therapist matching, session management
- **Self-Care**: Meditation, mindfulness, journaling, mood tracking
- **Crisis Support**: Immediate help resources, safety planning, emergency access

## Key Principles

1. **Do No Harm**: Avoid gamification, streaks, or pressure that creates guilt
2. **Privacy First**: Mental health data requires the highest protection standards
3. **Crisis-Ready**: Suicide prevention resources always visible and accessible
4. **Trauma-Informed**: Content warnings, opt-in approaches, user control
5. **Realistic Expectations**: No quick fixes or guaranteed outcomes
