# Productivity — Maintainers

This demographic is **not AAA-strict**. PRs require **one reviewer** from the maintainer list below.

## Teams

- **@skeed/maintainers** — general Skeed maintainers; can review any demographic PR
- **@skeed/productivity-domain-leads** — domain leads with specific expertise in productivity and knowledge-worker tooling

## Domain leads

- _vacant — apply via [contributing](../../../docs/contributing/add-a-demographic.md)_

## Review SLA

One reviewer approval required before merge. Domain leads are preferred reviewers for changes to `preset.json`, `psychology/`, and any new niche additions. Maintainers may review SVG primitives, illustration style, and copy changes.

## What this demographic covers

Apps for knowledge workers, freelancers, and teams aged 22–45. Niches: task management, project management, note-taking, time tracking, email clients, and focus tools. Anti-patterns include notification spam, streak shaming, social comparison pressure, fake urgency, and dark-pattern upsells.

## Subject-matter context for reviewers

- Familiarity with WCAG 2.1 AA contrast requirements.
- Understanding of flow state UX and keyboard-first interaction patterns.
- Awareness of dark patterns common in productivity SaaS (streaks, social pressure, notification overload).
- No regulatory specialisation required (unlike kids, health, legal, or gov demographics).
