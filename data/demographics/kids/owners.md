# Kids — Maintainers

This is an **AAA-strict** demographic. PRs require **two reviewers**, including at least one domain lead.

## Domain leads

- _vacant — apply via [contributing](../../../docs/contributing/add-a-demographic.md)_

## Subject-matter requirements for reviewers

- Familiarity with COPPA (US) and the UK Age-Appropriate Design Code.
- WCAG 2.1 AAA contrast and cognitive guidelines.
- Background in early-childhood UX, accessibility, or developmental psychology.

## What this demographic covers

Apps for ages 4–12. Learning, play, creativity, light social with full parental controls. Anti-patterns include behavioral nudges, infinite scroll, autoplay AV, third-party tracking, and any pattern that erodes privacy defaults.
