## Review SLA
**AAA-STRICT**: Requires TWO reviewers — one from @skeed/gov-domain-leads AND one from @skeed/a11y-reviewers. No exceptions.

## AAA-strict requirements
- WCAG 2.1 AAA (7:1 contrast minimum for all text)
- Section 508 compliant
- No motion (prefers-reduced-motion is the DEFAULT, not opt-in)
- Plain language at grade 8 reading level
- All interactive elements keyboard-navigable
- Tag PRs with `aaa-strict` and `gov-domain`
